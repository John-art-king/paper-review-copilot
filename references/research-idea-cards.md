# Research Idea Cards

Generate follow-up work from evidence, not fashion. Retain only ideas tied to a demonstrated limitation, untested assumption, conflicting result, or missing regime.

## Rejected Idea Patterns

Do not retain ideas that only say "use a larger model", "add attention", "try more data", or "combine A and B" without a mechanism and a falsification test.

## Idea Card

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Evidence-backed gap | What is missing and the source locator |
| Hypothesis | A statement that can be false |
| Intervention | Smallest method or experimental change that tests it |
| Decisive experiment | Data, baseline, control, and protocol |
| Metrics | Primary quality metric plus robustness or efficiency metrics when relevant |
| Positive result | Outcome that supports the hypothesis |
| Falsification | Outcome that rejects it |
| Contribution type | Method, theory, empirical finding, benchmark, or replication |
| Feasibility and risk | Data, compute, implementation, and evaluation constraints |

## Ranking

Rank retained ideas by evidence strength, expected research value, feasibility, and ability to distinguish competing explanations. Label each as a direct extension, replication or benchmark, or cross-domain transfer.
